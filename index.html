Welcome to my portfolio! This website contains of all of the projects I completed while taking GEOG 572 - Graphic Design in Cartography. While in this class, I learned about visual storytelling, visual cognition, visual semiotics and ethics in map design. I also got skills in using the suite of ESRI's ArcGIS products, specifically Storymaps, Fieldmaps and Guided Tours.
