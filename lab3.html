Lab 3

Stylized map created in Lab 3

In Lab 3, I had to create a web map tileset with a style based on 3 inspiration pieces of my choosing. The pieces I chose were posters and scenes from animated films & TV shows I have enjoyed watching recently. The posters were from Spiderman - Into the Spiderverse and Arcane, while the scene still was from Puss in Boots 2.

To create the tileset I used Mapbox Studio, which allowed me to customize every facet of the basemap to match my inspiration pieces. Adobe Illustrator was used to pick out colors from my inspriation pieces, along with incorporating a texture from one of the pieces as a background in the basemap.
