Lab 2

View from Grandad's Bluff, one of the stops | Link to image

For Lab 2, I created a guided tour of my hometown, La Crosse, WI, using ArcGIS' Guided Tour and Fieldmaps features. Each of the eight stops I felt was unique to La Crosse, or important enough such that a first time visitor should see. Using the Fieldmaps mobile app, I visited each stop and mapped my location, along with taking a picture and writing a brief description.
